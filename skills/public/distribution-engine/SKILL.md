# Distribution Engine — Founder OS

Builds owned, earned, and compound distribution infrastructure.

> "Distribution isn't something you add to your product. Distribution is your product."

---

## Why Distribution-First Is Now Essential

Three shifts have made this a survival necessity, not optional strategy:

**CAC Inflation Crisis**
- Paid CPMs up 40–70% vs 2022
- SaaS CAC payback stretched from 6–12 months to 18–30+ months
- Paid ads mask weak positioning — you avoid hard conversations with real users
- Alternative: Owned channels where each interaction compounds

**AI Content Multiplication**
- Billions of AI-generated pages published since 2023
- Generic content marketing is now table stakes, not differentiation
- Alternative: Original data, frameworks, and insider access that AI can't replicate

**Creator Economy Convergence**
- Every founder is now a media company by default
- Your product can be copied. Your community can't.
- Alternative: Build distribution moats alongside product moats

## The Distribution Triangle

Three interconnected vertices. Weakness in any one undermines the system.

```
           CREDIBILITY
          (Insight Assets)
              /\
             /  \
            /    \
           /      \
    AUDIENCE ———— CHANNEL
   (Trust Before   (Owned
   Transactions)  Distribution)
```

### Vertex 1: Audience-First

**The Depth Test**: Can you name 50 people in your target market who know your name and would take your call? If not: width without depth.

**Success signals:**
- People share your content without being prompted
- Prospects reference your content in sales conversations
- Industry peers engage with and amplify your work
- Competitors start copying your messaging

**Owned channel benchmarks to hit:**
- 30%+ of website traffic from owned channels
- Email open rates >25% (industry avg: 15–20%)
- Waitlist conversion >30% to active usage
- Community engagement without constant prompting

### Vertex 2: Channel-First

**Rented attention is fragile. Owned attention compounds.**

| Channel Type | Example | Control | Compounds? | Build Order |
|--------------|---------|---------|-----------|-------------|
| **Owned** | Email list, community, podcast | Full | Yes | Priority 1 |
| **Earned** | Creator mentions, PR, citations | Partial | Yes | Priority 2 |
| **Rented** | LinkedIn followers, Twitter | None | No | Vanity |
| **Paid** | Ads | Temporary | No | Last resort |

**Rule**: Every piece of content on any rented channel should capture into an owned channel. LinkedIn post → email capture. Twitter thread → newsletter CTA.

### Vertex 3: Credibility-First

Stop asking creators and communities to share your content. Become the upstream source they can't ignore.

**The Creator Decision Filter** (what any creator with a real audience asks):
"Will this make my audience smarter?"

**Passes the filter:**
- Benchmark data answering questions their audience keeps asking
- Frameworks that reorganize messy problems
- Research that contradicts what everyone assumes is true

**Fails the filter:**
- "Research" that's repackaged product marketing
- Frameworks that suspiciously require your product
- Data with no verifiable methodology

## Playbook 1: Waitlist Engineering

### Advanced Waitlist Architecture

**Three-layer waitlist design:**

| Layer | Mechanism | Goal |
|-------|-----------|------|
| Core waitlist | Email capture with specific promise | Build list |
| Referral loop | "Move up the list" viral mechanic | Make list grow itself |
| Content drip | Weekly insight → keeps warm → builds anticipation | Maintain quality |

**Referral loop prompt:**
```
Help me design a viral waitlist referral mechanic for [product].

Target customer: [ICP]
Core promise to waitlist: [what they're waiting for]
Incentive I can offer: [what moves them up / what they unlock]

Design:
1. The referral mechanic (how it works)
2. The copy for the share prompt
3. The email sequence (welcome → referral ask → weekly warm-up)
4. What milestone unlocks early access
5. How to make sharing feel like status, not spam
```

### Waitlist to Launch Conversion

Target: >30% of waitlist → active usage at launch.

Pre-launch sequence:
- **T-4 weeks**: Behind the scenes / build-in-public content
- **T-2 weeks**: "Founding member" framing + social proof from beta users
- **T-1 week**: Early access opens to top referrers first
- **Launch**: Full announcement with proof of waitlist size as social proof

## Playbook 2: The Give-First Ladder (Creator/Influencer Distribution)

Progressive relationship building. Skip steps = extraction signal. Everyone can tell.

### The 5 Steps

**Step 1: Silent Signal**
Share genuine insight assets with zero ask. No "let me know your thoughts." No collaboration mention. Pure value delivery.

**Step 2: Value Remix**
When they engage, offer deeper data or raw materials. Still no ask.

**Step 3: Attribution Exchange**
They share your content. Amplify their work. Reciprocity without transaction.

**Step 4: Collaborative Creation**
"We're researching X. Your perspective would make it stronger." Co-authoring, not sponsorship.

**Step 5: Ongoing Partnership**
Regular insight exchange. Mutual audience growth.

### The Insight Asset That Gets Shared

| Asset Type | Example | Shareability |
|-----------|---------|-------------|
| Benchmark data | "We analyzed 500 cold email campaigns..." | Very high |
| Contrarian framework | "Everyone says X. Here's why that's wrong..." | High |
| Proprietary research | Survey results from your ICP | Very high |
| Tool/calculator | Free tool solving a common problem | High |
| Curated database | Compiled resource list nobody else has made | Medium-high |

## Playbook 3: The Co-Marketing Growth Loop

### Why Co-Marketing Now

- Cold outreach response: 8–9% → 3–5%
- Partner-anchored outreach converts at 30–40% vs 1–5% cold
- Only ~12% of companies have a formal partner program = distribution arbitrage

### Partner-Market Fit Scoring (1–5 each)

| Criterion | Question |
|-----------|----------|
| **Audience overlap** | Do their customers match your ICP without direct competition? |
| **Trust transfer** | Do their customers trust their recommendations? |
| **Execution match** | Can they actually execute with you (team, processes)? |
| **Mutual value** | Clear benefit for both sides? |
| **Integration potential** | Product-level integration possible? |

**Score 18+** = strong partner candidate. Proceed to outreach.
**Score 12–17** = potential, validate further.
**Score <12** = skip.

### Co-Marketing Formats by Stage

| Stage | Best Format | Why |
|-------|-------------|-----|
| Foundation Builder | Warm intro reciprocity | Low lift, immediate trust transfer |
| PMF Seeker | Joint content (co-authored piece) | Audience reach without budget |
| Scale Prepper | Joint research report | Earned distribution at scale |
| Growth Optimizer | Integration launch + joint GTM | Product-level lock-in |

### Co-Marketing Outreach Template

```
Subject: [Your Company] × [Their Company] — simple idea

Hi [Name],

I've been following your work on [specific topic they cover].
Your [content/product/audience] and ours overlap in an interesting way.

Our ICP: [specific]
Yours: [what you understand theirs to be]
Non-competing because: [why]

One simple idea: [joint research / webinar / integration / content piece]

Value for your audience: [specific]
Value for our audience: [specific]

Would you want to explore? Even a 20-min call to see if there's something there.

[Your name]
```

## Playbook 4: LinkedIn GTM System

### The 5 Virality Archetypes

| Archetype | Core Mechanism | Best For |
|-----------|---------------|----------|
| **Relatability Loop** | "This happened to me too" → recognition → share | Building initial audience |
| **Controversy Catalyst** | Challenges conventional wisdom → debate → reach | Rapid audience growth |
| **Education Engine** | Teaches something genuinely useful → bookmarks → authority | Long-term credibility |
| **Social Proof Cascade** | Results, data, transformations → desire → pipeline | Converting audience to leads |
| **Insider Access** | Behind-the-scenes, raw numbers → trust → depth | Converting followers to advocates |

### The POV-to-Pipeline System

```
1. Define Point of View
   → Contrarian, specific, defensible
   → "The conventional wisdom is X. Here's why that's wrong based on [evidence]."

2. Build Content Operations
   → Document, don't create
   → Capture insights from customer calls, experiments, data

3. Engagement System
   → Respond to every comment within 1 hour for first 60 min after post
   → POV-consistent comments on others' posts

4. Demand Architecture
   → Profile optimized for inbound (clear ICP language in headline)
   → CTA to owned channel (newsletter, community, waitlist)

5. Attribution
   → Track: post → comment → DM → meeting → opportunity
   → The goal is pipeline, not vanity metrics
```

### Founder LinkedIn Content Calendar (2–3 hrs/month)

| Week | Post Type | Archetype |
|------|-----------|-----------|
| Week 1 | Customer story / insight | Social Proof Cascade |
| Week 2 | Contrarian take on your industry | Controversy Catalyst |
| Week 3 | Step-by-step framework / lesson | Education Engine |
| Week 4 | Build-in-public update / milestone | Insider Access |

## Playbook 5: AEO/GEO — AI Search Visibility

### What Gets Cited

**For ChatGPT/Claude citations:**
- Clear structure: headings, step-by-step blocks, numbered lists
- Schema markup on key pages
- Be the definitive answer to a specific question
- Original data and frameworks > opinion pieces

**For Google AI Overviews:**
- Community-validated content (Reddit, YouTube, forums)
- Fresh opinions from real practitioners
- Specific, opinionated takes outperform balanced overviews

**Universal rule**: Unique data gets cited. Repackaged content doesn't.

### AEO Content Blueprint

```markdown
Target query: [specific question your ICP asks AI tools]

Content requirements:
- Answer the question directly in first 2-3 sentences
- Provide step-by-step breakdown with clear headings
- Include data or specific examples (not just frameworks)
- Cover edge cases and "what about X?" questions
- End with your proprietary take or data

Schema to add: FAQ schema, HowTo schema where applicable

Distribution to build citations:
- Publish on your own domain (canonical source)
- Seed in relevant communities where practitioners discuss it
- Get linked from 3+ trusted sources in your space
```

- [ ] Creator/partner relationships at each step of Give-First Ladder
- [ ] Co-marketing assets live and performing
- [ ] AI search visibility (search your ICP's top questions in ChatGPT/Perplexity)

**Pipeline Attribution**
- [ ] % of pipeline from owned/earned channels
